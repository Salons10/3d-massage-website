# 3D Massage Website Build & Maintenance Proposal

This document breaks down the costs and ongoing value for the custom website built for your massage business.

## 1. Initial Website Build & Design

A professional, high-converting website isn't just about code; it's about strategy, design, and reliable technology. Building a custom React-based application is an investment in a robust digital storefront.

### Estimated Time Investment (~40 Hours Total)

To understand the cost, here is a transparent breakdown of the hours our team dedicated to bringing this project to life:

*   **Discovery, Planning & UI/UX Design (6 Hours):** 
    Understanding brand identity, curating colors/typography, and designing a premium, user-friendly layout.
*   **Custom Frontend Development (20 Hours):** 
    Writing the entire website from scratch using modern frameworks (React). This includes responsive design for mobile and desktop, complex interactive components, and smooth animations (like the "How It Works" section).
*   **Backend & API Integrations (8 Hours):** 
    Connecting external services to make the website functional. This includes configuring the Resend API for the contact form, connecting the Wix Bookings system, and setting up the hosting environment.
*   **Testing, Optimization & Launch (6 Hours):** 
    Cross-browser testing, mobile responsiveness checks, performance optimization, and final deployment.

### Build Cost Options

Based on the 40+ hours of custom development, here is the value of the build:

*   **At Base Cost ($50/hour): $2,000** 
    *(This covers the raw cost of our developers' time.)*
*   **Standard Agency Rate ($75/hour): $3,000** 
    *(This is a standard rate for custom web applications, factoring in project management and long-term codebase architecture.)*

**Recommended Client Quote for Build:** **$2,500 - $3,000**
*(We recommend settling in this range to ensure a fair profit margin while remaining highly competitive for a custom React application.)*

## 2. Monthly Maintenance & Support Plans

Technology changes constantly. Browsers update, APIs change versions, and security vulnerabilities emerge. A website needs ongoing maintenance to remain fast, secure, and functional. 

Furthermore, you will likely need text updates, price changes, or new images uploaded over time. Our maintenance plans ensure you never have to worry about the technical side of your business.

*(Note: These plans do NOT include the client's direct cost for their Wix Bookings subscription, which they pay directly to Wix).*

### Tier 1: Essential Hosting & Security
**Price: $99 / month**

Best for: Peace of mind that your website is online and protected.
*   **Premium Hosting:** Lightning-fast hosting via Vercel's global edge network.
*   **SSL Certificate:** Automatic renewal of your security certificate (the padlock icon in the browser).
*   **Security & Package Updates:** Monthly checks and updates to the underlying code libraries (React, Vite, etc.) to prevent security risks.
*   **Uptime Monitoring:** We monitor the site to ensure it never goes down. If it does, fixing it is on us.
*   *Note: Does not include text/image updates. Any requested website edits are billed at an hourly rate.*

### Tier 2: The "Growth" Package (Most Popular)

Best for: Active businesses that occasionally change prices, services, or staff.
*   *Includes Everything in Tier 1.*
*   **1.5 Hours of Content Updates Per Month:** Simply text or email us with changes. Want to update a massage price? Swap out a staff photo? Add a holiday hours banner? We handle it. No random invoices for simple changes.
*   **Priority Support:** Your requests jump to the front of the line, guaranteed turnaround within 24-48 hours for standard edits.

### Tier 3: The All-Inclusive Partner
**Price: $350 / month**

Best for: Businesses looking to actively grow and refine their digital presence over time.
*   *Includes Everything in Tiers 1 & 2.*
*   **Up to 3 Hours of Development/Updates:** Can be used for content changes or building small new features/sections over time.
*   **Quarterly Performance & SEO Audit:** Every 3 months, we review your website's traffic and search engine health, tweaking tags and content to keep you ranking well.
